Universal Music Group South Africa: “Kelly Khumalo"

Project Link

Directed and Produced From a God to a King is the tenth studio album by South African singer Kelly Khumalo, released in February 2023.

Goal:

The objective was to translate the album's themes into visually compelling music videos that reflected artistic depth, cultural identity, and emotional authenticity.

Challenge:

The primary challenge was delivering high-quality visual storytelling that remained true to the album's narrative while meeting tight production timelines and maintaining cinematic excellence across multiple music videos.

Outcome:

Cloud UKO successfully delivered visually cohesive, professionally directed music videos that strengthened the album's storytelling, elevated production quality, and reinforced the artist's creative vision.
