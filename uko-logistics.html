UKO Logistics

Connecting You to Trusted Experts. Simplifying Mining Operations. Ensuring Operational Excellence.

Specialising in mining logistics facilitation in the DRC, the services act as a reliable intermediary between mining companies and global service providers. All procurement, mining equipment supply, facility installation, and operational construction is professionally coordinated, connecting clients to the right, trustworthy partners with extensive years of experience. Full operational support is provided without clients needing to manage the heavy workload themselves.

Mining Truck purchase and rental Facilitation

Facilitating access to heavy-duty mining trucks, whether for purchase or rental, by connecting clients with trusted suppliers with proven expertise.

Services include:

  • Coordinating the purchase, lease, or rental of mining trucks
  • Connecting clients to verified suppliers and fleet rental companies with extensive experience
  • Managing delivery schedules, fleet mobilisation, and operational readiness
  • Ensuring trucks meet operational and safety requirements through partner verification
  • Advising on vehicle selection tailored to mining project needs

Clients gain seamless access to reliable mining trucks without handling procurement or supplier relationships directly.

Facilitation of Mining facility supply And Construction

Acting as a facilitator between mining companies and experienced construction and installation teams, ensuring facilities are built efficiently and safely.

Services include:

  • Connecting clients to suppliers and contractors for processing plants and support infrastructure
  • Coordinating supply, installation, and construction schedules
  • Ensuring compliance with safety, environmental, and operational regulations
  • Overseeing the power, water, and material handling system installation
  • Providing progress updates and reporting on behalf of clients

The facilitation role ensures mining companies receive fully operational facilities without managing the operational workload.

Facilitation of Mining Machinery Provision

Providing access to all types of mining machinery, from small processing units to industrial-scale plants and heavy-duty machines, through partners with extensive experience.

Services include:

  • Connecting clients to verified machinery suppliers
  • Coordinating installation, commissioning, and on-site support
  • Arranging delivery and operational readiness checks
  • Facilitating maintenance agreements and ongoing technical support
  • Advising on equipment selection and scalability for mining operations

Clients benefit from high-quality mining machinery while all supplier coordination is handled professionally.

Mining Logistics and Supply Chain Facilitation

Streamlined mining supply chain and logistics management ensure operational efficiency while reducing stress for mining companies.

Services include:

  • Organising transport and delivery of heavy machinery and materials
  • Connecting clients to experienced logistics providers
  • Managing site mobilisation, inventory, and warehousing
  • Overseeing on-site delivery, installation, and operational readiness
  • Streamlining multi-party operations for complex mining projects

Facilitation guarantees timely, efficient, and reliable logistics while clients focus on production.
